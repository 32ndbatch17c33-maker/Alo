About ALO

The Ultimate Multi-Utility Workspace Matrix System Details.

Welcome to ALO

Welcome to ALO, designed to simplify your digital life and bring ease to your daily workflow.

Named after its creator, John Carlo (affectionately known as "Alo"), the app draws inspiration from alon—the Tagalog word for waves. Just like riding the unpredictable waves of a busy day, modern life requires us to multitask seamlessly. Think of ALO as your digital octopus: a single, convenient hub that brings all your essential tools together in one place, helping you stay productive and flow effortlessly through your daily tasks.

Key Features (Version 1.0)

  • Grade Analyzer: Calculate your General Weighted Average (GWA) effortlessly, both for an individual semester and across your entire academic journey. It features a built-in line graph to help you visually analyze and track your academic performance over time.
  • Clock Suite: Stay focused and manage your time effectively with an integrated Timer and Stopwatch—perfect for boosting productivity using techniques like the Pomodoro method. It also includes a World Clock to track time across different time zones.
  • Daily Planner: Plot your tasks with specific dates and times, prioritize them by urgency, and easily toggle them as "complete" or "pending" to keep your goals on track.
  • Subjects Schedule Coordinator: A dedicated space to organize your classes, timings, and subjects so you never miss a lecture.

The Design

Immerse yourself in a workspace styled with a beautiful, calming Ocean Blue palette, inspired by the very waves that gave the app its name.

Privacy & Data Security

Your privacy is our utmost priority. ALO is built with a strict local-first architecture:

  • 100% Local Storage: All your data—including your grades, schedules, and planner entries—is stored exclusively on your own device.
  • No External Servers: No data is uploaded to the cloud, and no third party (including the developer) can ever view, access, or track your information.
  • Complete Control: Your data belongs entirely to you, ensuring a completely secure and private workspace.

About the Developer & Future Vision

ALO was created by John Carlo Perin, a BS Civil Engineering student from the University of the Philippines Los Baños (UPLB).

The app was born out of a desire to turn an unexpected free period during the midyear term into a fruitful, creative venture. Instead of letting a lack of midyear classes slow him down, John Carlo decided to dive headfirst into app development, building a practical tool to solve real-world challenges for students and productivity enthusiasts alike.

This is just the beginning. Version 1.0 lays the groundwork, and the goal is to continuously evolve ALO. By gathering insights, feature requests, and user feedback from friends and peers, the app will be continually refined to its fullest potential—serving as a powerful, all-in-one companion to help everyone navigate their busy lives and successfully ride the waves of life.
